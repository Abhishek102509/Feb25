About CDAC Mumbai

Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) is the premier R&D organization of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) for carrying out R&D in IT, Electronics and associated areas.

CDAC Mumbai is one of the most active centers of C-DAC engaged in cutting edge technology areas like High Performance Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, Health Informatics, Language Computing, etc.

The center has been instrumental in design, development and deployment of several state-of-the-art technologies and solutions for various sectors.
